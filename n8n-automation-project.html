Automating AI Video Creation & Publishing with n8n

A self‑hosted pipeline that generates AI‑powered videos, uploads to YouTube, and sends instant notifications to Telegram, Gmail, and Discord.

← Back to Projects Home
Overview

Content creation is exciting, but the repetitive process of editing, uploading, and sharing videos can be overwhelming. I built a fully automated system with n8n that takes a simple idea and transforms it into a published video with notifications sent everywhere I need them — saving time and ensuring consistency.

Why This Project Matters

Time is better spent on ideas than on repetitive publishing steps. This automation saves hours and ensures consistency: every video follows the same flow — creation, upload, and announcement — without manual work. It’s like having a personal content manager running 24/7.

What I Learned

  • Hosting and configuring n8n on‑prem for control and security.
  • Integrating AI models with the YouTube API for automated publishing.
  • Setting up cross‑platform notifications across Gmail, Telegram, and Discord.
  • Designing structured workflows that scale creative projects.
Final Reflection

This project proved that automation can amplify creativity instead of replacing it. By building this system, I freed up mental space to focus on ideas and strategy rather than manual uploads. Next steps: add analytics tracking and expand beyond YouTube.
