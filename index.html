Journey

Nepal to Sydney

A story that shaped how I approach technology, resilience, and human connection.

01
🏔️

Humble Beginnings

Born in Sankarpokhari, Kusma District — a remote village with no roads or electricity. The nearest city was a two-hour walk. My father, a Gurkha in the Indian Army, was our stepping stone.

02
📚

Growing Bigger

Moved to Kusma at five, then Chitwan for better schooling. Each move expanded my world. By high school I had a clear goal — go further for myself and everyone who believed in me.

03
🌏

Leap to Sydney

Moved to Sydney for an IT degree. Worked with Apple, Telstra, and Nike. Today I'm a Level 2 ICT Analyst delivering enterprise infrastructure solutions every day.
