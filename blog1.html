Personal Story

From Sankarpokhari
to Sydney

A journey of dreams, sacrifice, and growth — from a remote Nepalese village with no electricity, to enterprise IT in Australia.

Chapters

The Journey

01
🏔️

Humble Beginnings

Born in Sankarpokhari, Kusma District — a remote village with no roads or electricity. The nearest city was a two-hour walk. My father, a proud Gurkha in the Indian Army, was our stepping stone.

02
📚

A Path to Growth

Moved to Kusma at five, then Chitwan for better schooling. Each move stretched my world wider. By high school I had a clear goal — go further, for myself and everyone who believed in me.

03
🌏

The Leap to Sydney

Moved to Sydney for an IT degree. Worked with Apple, Telstra, and Nike. Today I serve as ICT Technical Support Specialist, Level 2 Analyst at Opal Healthcare.

"Your beginnings don't define you — your growth does."
— Anup Rijal
