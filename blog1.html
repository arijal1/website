Story

From Sankarpokhari to Sydney: A Journey of Dreams, Sacrifice, and Growth

A personal narrative of beginnings, courage, and continuous growth — presented with the same animated design as your home and blog pages.

Chapters

Humble Beginnings

I was born in Sankarpokhari, a small, remote village nestled in the hills of the Kusma District in Nepal. Life there was peaceful — but far from easy. We had no road access, no electricity, and the nearest city was a two‑hour walk away. Opportunity felt far off, and dreams had to climb hills of reality to even feel possible.

My father, a proud Gurkha serving in the Indian Army, worked tirelessly to support us. His sacrifice was our stepping stone.

Sankarpokhari • https://github.com/arijal1/website/blob/main/images/IMG_6156.jpeg

A Path to Growth

When I was five years old, we moved to Kusma so I could attend a better school. It was the start of a new chapter. After two years, our family moved again — to Chitwan — where I completed my schooling. It was there that my dreams grew bigger.

I knew I wanted to go further — for myself, and for the family who believed in me.

Kusma to Chitwan • https://github.com/arijal1/website/blob/main/images/images/IMG_3951.jpeg

A Leap of Faith

After high school, I moved to Sydney to pursue a degree in IT. It was a leap into the unknown — new country, new people, new culture. The transition was difficult, but it shaped who I am today.

I’ve now completed my studies, worked with global brands like Apple and Nike, and continue to grow both personally and professionally.
